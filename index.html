Curriculum

Workshop modules

Twelve intensive modules spanning molecular biology fundamentals through advanced sequencing and bioinformatics.

  1. 01Laboratory Biosafety & Biosecurity
  2. 02Introduction to Molecular Biology
  3. 03DNA/RNA Extraction
  4. 04PCR (Conventional)
  5. 05Real Time PCR (qRT-PCR)
  6. 06Sanger Sequencing
  7. 07Base Calling
  8. 08Next Generation Sequencing
  9. 09Bioinformatics
  10. 10Phylogenetic Analysis & Evolutionary Relationship
  11. 11Data Collation & Processing
  12. 12Instrumentation for Molecular Biology
